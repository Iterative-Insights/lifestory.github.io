LifeStory is an app about remembrance and introspection. A living family knowledgebase, with the goal of maintaining family bonds across the generations. Typical families have 3 generations of memory, and this is a sliding window, always progressing. I hope to build something meaningful and lasting, that can help families maintain a lasting archive for the future. Those interested in beta testing in the coming months can send me an email below.
